ILocalization UI copy

Institution-specific text that doesn't fit a more focused port — primarily the legal disclaimers in the About modal. Override to insert your institution's name, portal, and any legally required language.

Port key
"localization"
Consumed by
DisclaimerModal — shown on first load and accessible from the About menu
Scope rule
Credit labels → ICreditSystem. Institution names → IInstitution. Attribute labels → IAttributeSystem. This port is for everything else.

Contract

FieldTypePurpose
disclaimersstring[]Ordered list of bullet points shown in the About / Disclaimer modal. Rendered as an unordered list. Include unofficial-status language, degree audit authority, data freshness, and storage limitations.

Northeastern reference implementation

export default {
  disclaimers: [
    "This is NOT an official Northeastern University tool and is not affiliated
     with or endorsed by Northeastern.",
    "This does NOT replace your official degree audit. Always verify your plan
     with your academic advisor and through MyNEU / DegreeWorks.",
    "Course availability, prerequisites, credit hours, and NUpath designations
     may be outdated or incorrect.",
    "Your saved plan lives in your browser's localStorage only. Clearing
     browser data will erase it.",
    "Use at your own risk.",
  ],
};

Generic default

export default {
  disclaimers: [
    "This is an unofficial academic planning tool and is not affiliated with
     or endorsed by your institution.",
    "This does NOT replace your official degree audit. Always verify your plan
     with your academic advisor.",
    "Course availability, prerequisites, and credit values may change. Confirm
     with your official course catalog.",
    "Your saved plan is stored in your browser only. Clearing browser data will
     erase it.",
    "Use at your own risk.",
  ],
};

The generic default is institution-neutral. It's usable as-is if you don't need custom legal language — but you should override to at least insert your institution's name and portal/audit tool names.

Implementing for your institution

Replace the generic bullets with your institution's language. Recommended bullets:

  1. Unofficial status: "This is NOT an official [Institution Name] tool…"
  2. Degree audit authority: "Always verify with your advisor and through [Portal] / [DegreeAudit]."
  3. Data freshness: Mention that catalog data may be stale.
  4. Storage warning: localStorage is browser-local — clearing data erases the plan.
  5. Liability: "Use at your own risk."
✦
Use institution.portalName and institution.degreeAuditName from IInstitution as the authoritative source of those names. The localization strings currently inline them as literals for simplicity, but they should match.
